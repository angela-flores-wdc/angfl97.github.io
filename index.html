About Me

I love viewing Computer Science as a creative process as well as a technical one. Technology impacts nearly every facet of our lives, and it takes creative minds to take a new idea, flesh it out, and implement it in living, breathing code. I am constantly exposing myself to new languages and technologies, looking for the next challenge. As a Latina in computing, I strive to diversify the field and to uplift other underrepresented communities. We need diversity in order to facilitate creativity!
